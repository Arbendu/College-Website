B-tech

B. Tech stands for Bachelor of Technology. It is an undergraduate level programme that is pursued by science stream students after the completion of Class 12 Science. The four year programme is offered by engineering colleges across India including top colleges such as IITs and NITs.

MCA

MCA is a postgraduate course that trains the students in the various aspects of computer programs, application software, computer architecture, operating systems and many more. MCA Course duration is 2 years. The MCA admission will be done on the basis of merit or entrance examination.

M-tech

KGEC offers MTech courses in various engineering streams, ranging from classic engineering streams such as mechanical, civil, and production engineering to high-tech streams like automobile and aeronautical. The duration of the MTech program is 2 years, which is divided into 4 semesters.
